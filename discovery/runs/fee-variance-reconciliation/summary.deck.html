Fee-variance reconciliation

Discovery readout · synthetic · non-production

Problem

Fee variances surface too late

  • 3.8% of invoiced fee lines diverge from the contracted tariff
  • Only visible at the month-end close — leakage compounds for weeks
  • Three systems report three different variance totals
Today the trigger is the close, not the event.
2 / 5
Who & why

Reconciliation is done by hand

  • Analyst rebuilds variance in an end-user spreadsheet (EUC)
  • Finance carries the leakage and the rework
  • Governance cannot evidence fee accuracy to source
3 / 5
Feasibility

Governance says: observe and reconcile

  • Maps to DR-1 Data Quality (DR-1.1-003 / DR-1.3-001 / DR-1.4-001)
  • Controls CTRL-DQ-003 / 006 / 012 take residual risk to Very Low
  • Commercial data only — no PSU PII; BCBS 239 lineage to source
Read-only observability + reconciliation, not a new processing purpose.
4 / 5
Direction

A fee-variance reconciliation monitor

  • Surface open variances per invoice line, not at month-end
  • One reconciled figure across ledger, invoice, and contract
  • Accuracy lineage available on demand
Prototype is direction, not specification — delivery authors the build.
