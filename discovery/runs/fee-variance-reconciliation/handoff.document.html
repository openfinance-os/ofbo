Fee-variance reconciliation — delivery hand-off

Discovery run · fee-variance-reconciliation · synthetic data, non-production

Problem

For a reconciliation analyst who must ensure invoiced TPP fees match the contracted tariffs, fee variances are invisible until the month-end close and inconsistent across the ledger, the Hub invoice, and the contract.

This causes financial leakage that compounds for weeks, manual end-user-spreadsheet rework, and no lineage to evidence fee accuracy.
