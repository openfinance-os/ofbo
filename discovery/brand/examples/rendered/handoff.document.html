Problem

For a consent operations analyst who must ensure every PSU withdrawal is honoured and acknowledged within the bank's 5s default, revoke acknowledgement latency and lifecycle-state drift are invisible until a customer complains.

This causes silent SLA breaches, stale-state errors in care handling, and no audit-ready proof of timely withdrawal.
